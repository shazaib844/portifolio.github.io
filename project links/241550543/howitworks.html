What is Noisli?

Noisli is your digital place for focus. With Noisli, you can mix

and match different sounds to create your perfect sound environment. We offer 28

high-quality background sounds that you can play individually or combine. Adjust the

volume for each sound to customize your combinations and make them your own.

Besides crafting your own Combos, you can listen to carefully curated Playlists

for inspiration and explore new sound suggestions. Noisli also provides a Timer to help you

work in sessions and prevent burnout, along with a distraction-free Text Editor.

Why Noisli works?

Silence and loud noises are usually associated with danger, thus making us alert. It is the opposite of what we need when

we want to focus or relax.

Background noise on the other hand is a sound that doesn’t contain spikes in volume, that is very constant and rather predictable

keeping therefore our inner alarm systems calm.

Moreover, due to the high amount of different frequencies contained, background sounds have great masking abilities, reducing the

negative impact of sudden spikes in external noise in our environment. While listening to such background sounds, every additional

noise adds to the sounds you’re already hearing and doesn’t stand out so much anymore. Therefore, additional noises are blended in

and you don’t perceive them as disturbing as you normally would. This is also why music with lyrics can bring us easily out of focus,

especially when having to deal with tasks that involve writing or reading. Music tends to be more rhythmic and unpredictable and if it

contains lyrics, it will easily mess with your concentration and shift your focus to the lyrics instead of the task at hand.

Lastly, the right amount of background noise, not too quiet and not too loud, can also help you to think in a more abstract way enhancing

creative thinking.
